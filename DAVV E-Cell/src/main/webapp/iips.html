About Us

Dr.Suresh T.Silawat
(Principal)

The Department of Information Technology at Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya (DAVV) Institute of Professional Studies (IIPS) is a cornerstone of excellence in the realm of IT education and research. Our mission is to empower students with advanced knowledge and practical skills in Information Technology, enabling them to thrive in their careers and contribute meaningfully to technological advancements. With a strong focus on academic excellence and innovation, we envision ourselves as a premier center for IT education and research.
At the heart of our department lies a team of dedicated faculty members who are not only highly qualified but also bring extensive industry experience to the table. They are committed to providing students with a rich learning experience, nurturing their intellectual curiosity, and guiding them towards academic and professional success. Our faculty members are actively engaged in research endeavors, pushing the boundaries of knowledge in various IT domains and inspiring students to pursue their research interests.
DAVV IIPS is equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure and facilities, including modern laboratories, well-equipped classrooms, and access to cutting-edge software and hardware resources. This infrastructure supports our teaching and research activities, providing students with hands-on experience and practical exposure to real-world IT scenarios. Additionally, we foster a culture of collaboration and innovation, encouraging students and faculty members to engage in interdisciplinary research projects and industry partnerships.
In essence, the Department of Information Technology at DAVV IIPS stands as a beacon of excellence, dedicated to shaping the future leaders of the IT industry. With our focus on academic rigor, research excellence, and industry collaboration, we strive to empower students to become competent IT professionals and visionary leaders who will drive innovation and positive change in the world.
