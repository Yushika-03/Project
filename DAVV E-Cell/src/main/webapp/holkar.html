About Us

Dr.Suresh T.Silawat
(Principal)

Government Holkar Science College, Indore, established in 10 June 1891 , has been a pioneer in providing quality education in the field of science. We are committed to nurturing young minds and preparing them to face the challenges of tomorrow. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a conducive learning environment, we strive to excel in academics, research, and extracurricular activities.

Our institution is ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001:2015 certified, reflecting our dedication to quality education and environmental sustainability. Moreover, we take pride in our NAAC Accreditation with an "A++" Grade in the 4th cycle, a testament to our relentless pursuit of excellence.

At Holkar Science College, we believe in holistic development, encouraging our students to explore their interests beyond the classroom. Through various clubs, societies, and cultural events, we provide ample opportunities for students to showcase their talents and develop essential life skills.

Join us at Government Holkar Science College, where knowledge meets innovation, and together, we shape the future.

Today, under the able guidance of Principal Dr. Suresh T. Silawat, the institution has scaled newer heights in all round development.

In its nascent years, the institution offered a diverse range of courses encompassing English Literature, Sanskrit, Persian, Logic, History, Political Economy, Mathematics, and Science. It aimed at the holistic development of students, many of whom played pivotal roles in the Indian freedom struggle.

Today, Holkar Science College continues its tradition of providing quality education to over 12,000 students.

The college also houses distance learning Study Centers for IGNOU, MPBOU, Mahatma Gandhi Chitrakoot Gramodaya Vishwavidyalaya.
